BAU-Enhancement-ACW-Liability - Include Limit of Liability in Payload for Liability flow-Copy of ACW-59



Description

Currently, the blanket Limit of Liability is not being sent in the payload.
Additionally, Blanket Deductibles are also not being sent in the payload. 


This story is to ensure that Both the correct Limit of Liability and Blanket Deductible value is captured and passed in the payload from EzDocs. 

Acceptance Criteria: 

Limit of Liability field is added to payload.

Blanket Deductibles field is added to the payload. 

Both Value is correctly fetched from source.

Payload reflects accurate and consistent data for both fields. 

No dependency on derived logic.
